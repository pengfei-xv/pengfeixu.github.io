About

I am currently an Assistant Professor with the VCC group at Shenzhen University. Before joining SZU, I worked with Prof. Chiew-Lan Tai as a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at Hong Kong University of Science and Technology, where I also obtained my Ph.D in Computer Science in 2015, under the guidance of Prof. Chiew-Lan Tai and Prof. Hongbo Fu. Before that, I received Bachelor's degree in Mathematics from Zhejiang University in 2009. My research falls in the fields of Computer Graphics and Human Computer Interaction, especially automatic/interactive 2D and 3D content creation.

I am currently looking for master students. Please contact me if you are interested in my research.
